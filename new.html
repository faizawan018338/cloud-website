Celebrating Culinary Excellence Since 2010

Founded by Chef Michael Anderson, Gourmet Haven began as a small bistro with a big dream - to bring exceptional dining experiences to our community. What started as a 10-table restaurant has grown into an award-winning culinary destination.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest ingredients, let their natural flavors shine, and present them with creativity and care. We work closely with local farmers and producers to ensure the freshest seasonal offerings.
